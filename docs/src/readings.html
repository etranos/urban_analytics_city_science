Key readings

All lecture slides include a reference list. Please go through these materials.

A indicative list of key readings include:

  • Bettencourt (2021)

  • Neal and Rozenblat (2021)

  • Light and Moody (2020)

  • Batty (2013)

  • Lovelace, Nowosad, and Muenchow (2019)

  • Singleton, Spielman, and Folch (2017)

Also, the Big Book of R is a great collection of R related books. Some of these include:

  • Wickham and Grolemund (2016) for tidyverse

  • Bauer, Landesvatter, and Behrens (2022) on how to access APIs through R

  • Wickham (2016) for visualisations with ggplot2

  • Xie, Dervieux, and Riederer (2020) for RMarkdown

  • Boehmke and Greenwell (2019)

Please don’t limit your reading in the following list.
